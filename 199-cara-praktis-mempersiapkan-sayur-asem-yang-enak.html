Sayur asem or sayur asam is an Indonesian vegetable soup. It is a popular Southeast Asian dish, consisting of vegetables in tamarind soup. Common ingredients are peanuts, young jackfruit, young leaves and unpeeled seeds of melinjo, bilimbi, chayote, long beans.
